About Us

Satyanam Energy is India’s leading solar power company, specializing in a wide range of renewable energy solutions. Our comprehensive product portfolio includes Solar Rooftop PV Systems, Solar Thermal Solutions, Solar Water Pumps, Solar Trees, Solar High Masts, and Solar Street Lights, serving a diverse clientele that includes residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

In the realm of Solar Rooftop PV Systems, we take pride in offering comprehensive Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) services for on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid systems. Our commitment to quality and reliability ensures that our clients across all sectors receive turnkey solutions they can trust. We firmly believe that the evolving landscape of solar technology enables us to deliver cost-effective energy solutions through our PV systems.

At Satyanam Energy, we recognize the pivotal role of research and development in driving progress in every industry. That’s why we’ve established a dedicated Research & Development Wing, comprising passionate experts who tirelessly work to innovate and create cutting-edge solar energy products. Our team of dedicated engineers specializing in solar energy domain are well-equipped to design and tailor solar energy products to meet the unique energy needs of our diverse clientele. This approach allows us to efficiently address the growing demand for clean, economical energy solutions in various sectors.

With a strong commitment to sustainability, quality, and innovation, Arin Energy is dedicated to leading the way in the solar energy sector. Our goal is to empower individuals, businesses, and industries with affordable, reliable, and environmentally-friendly energy solutions.
